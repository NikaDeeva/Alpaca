What is the value of alpaca wool?

Color range

Alpaca wool comes in 24 natural shades, from pure white, traditional beige or silver to brown and even black.

Hypoallergenic

Alpaca wool fiber does not contain lanolin, making it hypoallergenic.

Designers' love

Designer Giorgio Armani used alpaca suri wool in fashionable men's and women's suits.

Sportman`s love

Sports enthusiasts recognize that alpaca products are lighter and warmer, more comfortable in cold weather, so sportswear and outerwear manufacturers are starting to buy more alpaca products.

Quality products

The main advantage of alpaca wool products is that they practically do not form pellets - long fibers prevent felting

Subtlety and lightness

It does not retain water, is warm even when wet, and can effectively resist solar radiation.

Strength

Alpaca wool is three times stronger and seven times warmer than sheep's wool.
