Why buy an alpaca?

  • Valuable wool

    It is distinguished by its purity, fine fiber and strength.

  • Simple care and maintenance

    It is necessary to provide them with food, water and build a pen with a canopy or provide other shelter (clean, unheated room) in case of bad weather.

  • Alive toy

    Alpacas get along wonderfully with people, can participate in games with disabled children, and brighten up the loneliness of elderly people.

  • Friendly and cute pet

    Alpaca is a cute and funny animal that you can spend time with, forgetting about your problems and worries. By purchasing an alpaca, you acquire a positive friend who can give you a smile every day.
